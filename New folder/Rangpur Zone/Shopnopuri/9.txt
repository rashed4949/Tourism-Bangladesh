Shopnopuri is one of the most beautiful
 artificial tourist spot in North Bengal.
 You can go there either from Hili or from Dinajpur.
 There you can enjoy your full day
 watching this artificial beauty.
You can spend night also In Shoponopuri
 rest house but for that
 you will have to book in advance.
 It is an idle Picnic spot also
 where lots of picnic parties come every year
