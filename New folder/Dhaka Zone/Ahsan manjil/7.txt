Built in 1872 and standing on
 the river Buriganga,
 this stately building offers
 the visitors a feeling
 of the life-style
 of the Nawabs of Dhaka.
 Sometimes known as the Pink Palace,
 this building now houses
 a splendid museum.
 Basically, it was the residence
 of the Nawabs. 
Nawab Abdul Gani renovated this
 building in the year 1872 and
 named it after his son Khaza
 Ahasanullah.
