Shilaidaha Kuthibari, the famous residence
 of Rabindranath Tagore,
 is located at Kumarkhali.
Kumarkhali is an Upazila
 of Kushtia District 
in the Division of Khulna,
 Bangladesh.
 It is famous for the 
Shelaidaha area,
 where the poet Rabindranath
 Tagore spent a considerable time 
of his youth.
