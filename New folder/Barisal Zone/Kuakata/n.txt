Kuakata, locally known as Sagar Kannya 
(Daughter of the Sea) is a rare
 scenic beauty spot on the southernmost
 tip of Bangladesh.
 Kuakata in Latachapli union 
under Kalapara Police Station
 of Patuakhali district
 is about 30 km in length and 6 km 
in breadth. It is 70 km from Patuakhali 
district headquarters and 320 km from Dhaka.
